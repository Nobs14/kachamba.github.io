About Us

The Kachok Ambassadors Chorus (Kachamba Chorus) is a gospel music group found at SDA Kachok Church, Central Nyanza Conference, Kisumu South District, in Kisumu County Kenya. In recent years, their music has been a blessing to many resulting in a vast following, leading to an increase in its membership.
