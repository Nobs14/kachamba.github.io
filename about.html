About Us

The group is guided by key leaders, including Pr. Solomon Gilberts who is the Patron and Mrs. Lillian Solomons as the Matron. Elder Fred Omwata (Elder in Charge), Elder Elijah Kodek (Director), Mrs. Maureen Adika (Assistant Director), and Job Adams (Instructor & Music Coach). Additionally, the members are led by a vibrant executive committee, chaired by Cheryl Adhiambo and assisted by Eugene Marvel. The group also has a highly proficient team managing their social media presence, led by Allan George and Caxtone Ochieng.

A crucial part of the group's success comes from its dedicated sponsors, the Kachamba Sponsors Association (KSA), who play a key role in managing the group and ensuring its stability, growth, and continued impact in gospel ministry. The association is chaired by Elder Zakayo Okoth.

Our Mission

The Kachok Ambassadors are dedicated to meeting the spiritual, social, and lifestyle needs of youth aged 16-21. They challenge young people to experience and share a personal relationship with Christ, develop a lifestyle aligned with their beliefs and vocational interests, and engage in wholesome activities that foster lifelong relationships.
